The 20 / 20 Challenge

The 20 / 20 Challenge is a competition and training camp for those 20 and younger in Shreveport - Bossier, who have the next great business idea.

How does it work?

You can apply by clicking the link further down this page. Applications are accepted until June 1st, 2014. If our panel of judges picks you to participate, then we will notify you in the first week of June.

The Challenge

Entrepreneurial
Training

Everyone who is selected to participant in The 20 / 20 Challenge program will take part in a set of 4 Summer workshops that will teach them the basics of entrepreneurship and running a business.

Long-term
Mentorship

Each person that goes through The 20 / 20 Challenge program will be matched with a long-term mentor to help guide their future business endeavors over a 4 - 12 month period.

Cash
Prizes

The top 3 entrepreneurs that go through The 20 / 20 Challenge program will be selected by our panel of judges to receive modest cash prizes to kickstart their business!
